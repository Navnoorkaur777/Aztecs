"One Child, One Teacher, One Book and One Pen can change the World" - Malala Yousafzai

Adoption comes with many amazing benefits not only for the birth parents and adoptive couple but for the kids, too. Adoption provides children with the opportunity to experience life in a way that their birth parents wanted for them.And while there are certainly challenges along the way in raising an adopted child, it is important to remember that there are challenges in parenthood in general. It’s also important to recognize that the positive effects of adoption on the child far outweigh the negatives.
Adoption provides the child with a stable, loving home environment. Adopted children are often more likely to have better educational support and personal success. Adoptive parents want to see their child succeed in every way — emotionally, educationally, mentally, physically, and spiritually. They help them reach goals, and they often provide them learning opportunities that help enrich them and tap into their natural abilities. In the end, that translates to a child who grows into an adult who knows who they are and what they want out of life. Primary in this is growing up knowing the truth that they were adopted, chosen out of love. The birth mother, adoptive parents, and the adopted child can form a bond that lasts a lifetime. It provides the child with more love. It also provides the child with a chance to fulfill their dreams. Which creates a special space for the child to grow and thrive. They are given the chance to see and experience the world in a way that may not have happened without the adoption. This allows them to begin dreaming, even from a young age.
